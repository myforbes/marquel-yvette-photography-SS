Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical session take?
Most professional sessions last about one hour, which includes time to review images and make sure we have several strong options.
Do you help with posing and expression?
Yes. I guide you through every pose and help you find expressions that look relaxed, confident, and natural so you never feel unsure.
When will I receive my final images?
Proofs are typically delivered within two business days. Once you choose your favorites, final retouched images are delivered quickly after that.
Can you photograph my leadership team or staff?
Yes. I offer custom workplace and team headshot options. Share a few details about your team size and location and I will provide a tailored quote.
