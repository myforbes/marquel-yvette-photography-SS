1

Schedule Your Session

Contact me for a custom quote based on your team size. We'll find a date that works for your schedule—I'm flexible and can accommodate multiple days if needed for larger teams.

2

I bring all professional equipment to your location. Setup takes about 30 minutes in a conference room or designated space. Your team continues working until it's their turn.
